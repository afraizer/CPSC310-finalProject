Top of India

This place is fantastic! Great food and great location. I got my food to go, but the place looked very cozy. They have limited seating indoors so I would recomned calling ahead or getting your food deliveered. I will be back for sure!

King of Ramen

This is a great place for Vegatarians! They have many options for those who love spicy food, or if you like your food more mild. Definitly my favorite Ramen place in Spokane!

Le Brothers

Fast service and huge portions! Would recomend the Pho or any other Vietnamese dish. It's a little expensive but the food is definitly worth it and the food is made very fresh. They don't offer any outdoor dinning so it's all to-go for the time being.

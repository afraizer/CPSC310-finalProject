Le Brothers

Fast service and huge portions! Would recomend the Pho or any other Vietnamese dish. It's a little expensive but the food is definitly worth it and the food is made very fresh. They don't offer any outdoor dinning so it's all to-go for the time being.

NUDO

By far the best ramen resturant in Spokane! When I first pulled up I was worried because of how the outside looked, but the food more than makes up for that. The prices are pretty good for the amount of food you get, and they have a happy hour that is amazing. Would recomend to anyone who loves Ramen!
